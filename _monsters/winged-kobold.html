Kobold

Winged Kobold

Small humanoid (kobold), lawful evil
Armor Class 13 (leather armor, shield)
Hit Points 7
Speed 30 ft., fly 30 ft.

STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
7 (-2) 16 (+3) 9 (-1) 8 (-1) 7 (-2) 8 (-1)

Senses darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 8
Languages Common, Draconic
Challenge 1/4 (50 xp)

Sunlight Sensitivity. While in sunlight, the kobold has disadvantage on attack rolls, as well as on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight.

Pack Tactics. The kobold has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the kobold's allies is within 5 feet of the create and the ally isn't incapacitated.

Actions

Dagger.

  • Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft, one target
  • Hit: 5 (1d4 + 3) piercing damage

Dropped Rock.

  • Ranged Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, one target directly below the kobold
  • Hit: 6 (1d6 + 3) bludgeoning damage
